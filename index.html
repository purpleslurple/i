Drag this link to your bookmarks bar, then click it on an article page.

Reader View

Install

  1. Show your browser bookmarks bar.
  2. Drag Reader View above into the bookmarks bar.
  3. Open an article and click the bookmark.

If dragging is blocked or Safari strips the code, create any bookmark, edit it, then paste the code below into the bookmark's URL/location field.

Notes

This is a best-effort reader mode with PurpleSlurple paragraph anchors. It works on many article pages, but paywalled pages, apps that render content after login, browser extension pages, PDFs, and strict browser-internal pages can still block bookmarklets.
